Secondary Education Examination (SEE)

Class 10 – Science

Model Question Paper

Time: 3 Hours

Full Marks: 75

Pass Marks: 30

Instructions:

  • All questions are compulsory unless stated otherwise.
  • Attempt the required number of questions in each section.
  • Figures in the margin indicate full marks.
  • Draw neat and labelled diagrams wherever necessary.

Section A – Multiple Choice Questions 10 × 1 = 10

  1. Which of the following is responsible for carrying genetic information?
    a) Ribosome
    b) DNA
    c) Mitochondria
    d) Lysosome
  2. The unit of electric power is:
    a) Volt
    b) Ampere
    c) Watt
    d) Ohm
  3. The pH value of a neutral solution is:
    a) 0
    b) 7
    c) 14
    d) 1
  4. Which lens is used to correct myopia?
    a) Convex lens
    b) Concave lens
    c) Bifocal lens
    d) Cylindrical lens
  5. Which gas is produced during photosynthesis?
    a) Carbon dioxide
    b) Oxygen
    c) Nitrogen
    d) Hydrogen
  6. The acceleration due to gravity on Earth is approximately:
    a) 9.8 m/s²
    b) 10 m/s²
    c) 8 m/s²
    d) 9 m/s
  7. Which of the following is a renewable source of energy?
    a) Coal
    b) Petroleum
    c) Solar energy
    d) Natural gas
  8. The process of conversion of liquid into vapour is called:
    a) Condensation
    b) Evaporation
    c) Sublimation
    d) Freezing
  9. Which blood cells help in clotting?
    a) RBC
    b) WBC
    c) Platelets
    d) Plasma
  10. Which theory explains the origin of the universe?
    a) Cell theory
    b) Big Bang theory
    c) Atomic theory
    d) Evolution theory

  5. What is inertia?
  6. Name the main ore of iron.

Section C – Short Answer Questions 14 × 2 = 28

  1. Prove that the unit of electric resistance (ohm) can be expressed in fundamental units.
  2. Write any two characteristics of amphibians.
  3. Differentiate between somatic chromosome and sex chromosome (any two points).
  4. Draw the Punnett square for a monohybrid cross between heterozygous tall plants.
  5. Write any two causes of low white blood cell count.
  6. State any two effects of gravity.
  7. A stone is dropped from a height of 45 m. Calculate the time taken to reach the ground.
  8. What is the rate of reaction? Mention two factors affecting it.
  9. Write the IUPAC name of CH₃–CH₂–OH.
  10. Explain why sea water has higher density than river water.
  11. Differentiate between concave and convex lens (any two points).
  12. What is anomalous expansion of water?
  13. Write two differences between series and parallel circuits.
  14. Define catalyst with example.

Section D – Long Answer Questions 7 × 4 = 28

  1. Describe laboratory preparation of ammonia gas with labelled diagram and chemical equation.
  2. Derive the formula for kinetic energy and solve a related numerical problem.
  3. Explain Mendel’s law of segregation with suitable example and diagram.
  4. Describe causes, effects and control measures of air pollution.
  5. Explain working principle of transformer with diagram.
  6. Describe cloning process and mention two ethical issues related to it.
  7. Explain structure and working of human heart with labelled diagram.
